Rules:
- The red square are the zombies (health is between 1->100, speed is between 0.25->1) - they can change any white square into red square if they collide

- The white square are the humans (damage done to zombies is 50) - they can only damage the red square if they are inside a gray square

- The grey square are meant to represent houses, if they light up it means it's occupied by a white square
